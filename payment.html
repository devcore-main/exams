Refund Policy // fair & transparent

We offer partial refunds before development begins. Once a milestone is delivered and approved, that portion is non-refundable. We work to resolve any dissatisfaction before issuing refunds.

Invoice & Receipts // always provided

A formal invoice is issued for every transaction. Receipts are sent immediately upon payment confirmation. All transactions are documented for your records.

Currency // flexible

We accept EGP, USD, and EUR. Prices in USD are listed as reference — final pricing in your local currency will be confirmed before project start.

Security // your data is safe

We never store payment information. All transactions go through trusted third-party processors. A signed contract is provided for all projects above $200.
